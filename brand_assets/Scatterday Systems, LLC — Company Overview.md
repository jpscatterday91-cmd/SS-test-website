## Company Summary

Scatterday Systems is a Utah-based residential technology integrator specializing in the design, installation, configuration, and ongoing support of modern home technology systems. From high-performance structured cabling and enterprise-grade Wi-Fi to full smart home automation and premium AV, we deliver systems that are reliable, elegant, and built to last.

We work with homeowners, builders, and general contractors on everything from new construction pre-wire to retrofit upgrades — serving clients who expect their technology to simply work.

## Core Services

### 1. Consultation & Network Design
*The foundation of every great install.*

- Needs assessment and discovery conversations
- On-site survey and blueprint review
- Cable run planning and zone mapping
- Network architecture design and documentation
- Conduit and pathway planning for future-proofing
- Coordination with general contractors, electricians, and architects

---

### 2. Structured Cabling & Infrastructure
*Clean, certified, and built for the long haul.*

- Ethernet cable runs (Cat6/Cat6A) throughout the home
- In-wall and in-ceiling routing with clean finish
- Termination, patch panels, and port labeling
- Cable certification and testing documentation
- Server rack installation and professional cable management
- UPS, switch, router, and firewall configuration

---

### 3. Wi-Fi & Wireless Connectivity
*Full-home coverage. No dead zones.*

- Enterprise-grade access point selection, placement, and hardwiring
- VLAN segmentation for security and performance (IoT, guest, primary)
- Coverage validation and speed testing post-install
- Mesh and multi-AP configuration for large or complex floorplans
- Ongoing wireless optimization and expansion support

---

### 4. Security & Surveillance
*Peace of mind — always on.*
