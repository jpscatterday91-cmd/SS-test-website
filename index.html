What We Do

Everything your home needs to run beautifully.

From structured cabling and Wi-Fi to whole-home audio, home theatres, smart lighting, motorized shades, and surveillance — we design and install it all. Every project starts with a plan.

Consultation & System Design

We assess your needs, review blueprints, and build a complete system design before a single cable is run. Every great install starts with a great plan.

Structured Cabling & Wi-Fi

Professional ethernet runs and networking rack builds — paired with enterprise-grade access points hardwired throughout. Full-home coverage, no dead zones, no guesswork.

Audio & Video

Whole-home audio, dedicated home theaters, in-ceiling and in-wall speaker wiring, TV mounting, projectors, and screens. One-touch control or zone-by-zone — whichever fits the way you live.

Lighting Control

Beautiful light, thoughtfully controlled. Smooth, flicker-free dimming with scenes for dinner, movies, and morning routines. Scheduled, occupancy-triggered, or voice-activated — and integrated seamlessly with your smart home.

Motorized Shades

The right light, the right privacy, the right view — exactly when you want it. Motorized shades that raise at sunrise, close at sunset, and pair with your lighting scenes for the perfect atmosphere, any time of day.

Security & Surveillance

PoE IP cameras positioned for maximum coverage, with NVR and local or cloud storage. Motion zones, real-time alerts, and remote viewing — peace of mind, always on.

Smart Home & Automation

Custom automation scenes, smart thermostats, and air quality sensors — all tied together with your integrated control system. Your home responds to you, not the other way around.

Ongoing Support & Maintenance

Firmware updates, remote monitoring, and priority service calls. We don't disappear after install day — support plans mean you always have someone to call.
